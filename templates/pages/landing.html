About Solution

Communication is a very important aspect withing creatures. From basic understanding, relation and sharing vital information and secrets. We need to:

  • Have a common language.
  • Understand the method of communication.
  • Be able to express out intentions with the said form.

PidginEnglish bridges the gap between locals in the community that only understand and speak pidgin and any other person who doesn't have a mastery of the local language. It focuses on translating either

  • Pidgin to any language or
  • Any foriegn language to pidgin.
